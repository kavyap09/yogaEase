Yoga During Your Period: Find Your Balance

Yoga is a gentle way to ease cramps and stress during your period. Perfect for those with mild
discomfort or anyone seeking relaxation, poses like Balasana,Baddha Konasana can provide relief. Listen to your body, focus on gentle movements, and nurture yourself through your cycle!

Strengthen your body- simple and effective yoga poses
Bhujangasana(Cobra Pose)

The cobra pose helps you open your chest, strengthen your back, and stretch your abdominal muscles.

Balasana (Child's pose)

This beautiful asana helps regulate menstruation and aids in better functioning ovaries.

Baddha Konasana (Butterfly pose)

Resting posture that helps release tension in the body.

Malasna (Garland Pose)

Improves hip flexibility, strengthens the lower body, and aids in digestion by stimulating abdominal organs.

Adho Mukha Svanasana(Downward-Facing Dog)

Stretches the entire back, hamstrings, and calves, helping to lengthen the spine and strengthen the back muscles.

Dhanurasana (Bow Pose)

Strengthens the back muscles, improves posture, promoting flexibility and energy flow
