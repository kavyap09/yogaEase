Neck lengthening

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Ear to Shoulder: Take your right ear towards your right shoulder and pause.
  • Adjust Stretch: Gently explore where you need the stretch by moving your chin slightly up or down.
  • Extend Arm: Diagonally extend the left arm so that the fingertips touch the floor.
  • Hand Placement: Bring your right hand to the left side of your head and apply light pressure, feeling the stretch along the left side of your neck.
  • Resistance and Breathing: Gently resist the pressure of your hand with your head and breathe in and out slowly to a count of ten.
  • Other Side: Repeat the process on the other side.

What to avoid while doing this pose
  • Ensure you maintain a comfortable and relaxed position throughout the stretch.
  • Avoid applying excessive pressure with your hand; the stretch should be gentle.
  • If you experience any pain or discomfort, stop the stretch and consult a healthcare professional.
  • Do not force your head or arm into any position; let the stretch be natural and gradual.
