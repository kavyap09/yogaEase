Gomukhasana(Cow-Face Pose)

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Seated Position: Come into a comfortable seated position.
  • Left Elbow: Raise your left elbow and bend your arm so your hand comes to your back.
  • Right Hand Assistance: Use your right hand to gently pull your left elbow over to the right, or bring your right hand up to reach and hold your left hand.
  • Hold: Remain in this pose for 30 seconds.
  • Other Side: Then do it on the other side.

What to avoid while doing this pose
  • Ensure you are sitting on a comfortable surface and your spine is straight.
  • Avoid forcing your hands to clasp if you can't reach; use a strap or towel if needed.
  • If you feel any discomfort in your knees or shoulders, ease out of the pose and adjust as necessary.
  • Focus on even breathing and maintain a gentle stretch without straining.
