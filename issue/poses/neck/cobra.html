Bhujangasana

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Start Position: Lie face down on your mat with your legs extended and the tops of your feet pressing into the floor.
  • Hand Placement: Place your hands under your shoulders with your elbows close to your body.
  • Lift Chest: On an inhale, press into your hands and lift your chest off the ground, keeping your elbows slightly bent and shoulders away from your ears.
  • Hold the Pose: Engage your back muscles and hold the pose for 15 to 30 seconds, breathing deeply.
  • Release: Exhale and slowly lower your chest back to the floor. Rest and repeat if desired.

What to avoid while doing this pose
  • Ensure your shoulders are relaxed and away from your ears.
  • Avoid putting pressure on your lower back; engage your back muscles to lift your chest.
  • If you experience any discomfort in your lower back or wrists, ease out of the pose and adjust as needed.
  • Do not overextend your back; keep the movement smooth and controlled.
