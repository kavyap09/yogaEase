Uttana Shishosana (Puppy Pose)

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Start Position: Begin on all fours with your hands directly under your shoulders and your knees under your hips.
  • Walk Hands Forward: On an inhale, walk your hands forward while keeping your hips over your knees. Extend your arms as far as comfortable.
  • Lower Chest: Lower your chest towards the floor, allowing your forehead or chin to rest on the mat.
  • Hold the Pose: Keep your arms extended and your hips high, holding the pose for 30 seconds to 1 minute, breathing deeply.
  • Release: To come out of the pose, walk your hands back towards your knees and return to the all-fours position. Rest if needed.

What to avoid while doing this pose
  • Keep your hips aligned over your knees and avoid letting them sag or shift too much.
  • Ensure your shoulders stay relaxed and away from your ears.
  • If you experience any discomfort in your shoulders or lower back, ease out of the pose and adjust as needed.
  • Do not force your chest or forehead to the floor; let the stretch be gentle and gradual.
