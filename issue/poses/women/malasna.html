Baddha Konasana (Butterfly pose)

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Stand with your feet slightly wider than hip-width apart and toes pointing outward.
  • Slowly bend your knees and lower your hips into a deep squat, keeping your heels on the ground.
  • Bring your palms together in a prayer position (Anjali Mudra) at your chest.
  • Use your elbows to gently press your knees apart, opening your hips.
  • Keep your spine tall, chest lifted, and gaze forward.
  • Breathe deeply and hold the pose for 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  • To release, straighten your legs and come back to a standing position.
