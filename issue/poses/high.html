Ashwa Sanchalanasana (High lunge)

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Start in a standing position and step one foot back, bending the front knee to lower your hips.
  • Keep your front knee directly above your ankle and extend your back leg straight behind you.
  • Raise your arms overhead, keeping your shoulders relaxed and your torso upright.
  • Hold the pose for at least 20 seconds, then switch sides.

What to avoid while doing this pose
  • Avoid letting your front knee extend past your toes; keep it aligned over your ankle.
  • Ensure your back leg is straight and not hyperextended; avoid collapsing into the lower back.
  • Keep your shoulders relaxed and avoid tensing them up towards your ears.
