Balasana (Child's pose)

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Start by kneeling on the floor with your big toes touching and knees spread apart.
  • Sit back on your heels and then bend forward, extending your arms out in front of you on the floor.
  • Rest your forehead on the floor and relax your shoulders, allowing them to move away from your ears.
  • Hold this position for at least 20 seconds, breathing deeply and relaxing into the pose.

What to avoid while doing this pose
  • Avoid forcing your forehead to the floor; use a block or cushion if needed.
  • Do not place too much weight on your knees; ensure they are comfortably supported.
  • Keep your shoulders relaxed and avoid tensing them; let them fall naturally away from your ears.
