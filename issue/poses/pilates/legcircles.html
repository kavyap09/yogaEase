Leg Circles

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Lie on your back with your arms at your sides, legs extended, and feet flexed.
  • Lift one leg toward the ceiling, keeping it straight and aligned with your hip.
  • Inhale as you lower the leg across your body, then exhale as you circle it around and back to the starting position.
  • Complete 5-10 circles in one direction, then reverse the direction.
  • Repeat with the other leg.

What to Avoid When Doing the Bridge Yoga Pose
  • Arching the back: Keep your lower back pressed into the mat for stability.
  • Using momentum: Focus on slow, controlled movements rather than swinging your leg.
  • Lifting the opposite leg: Keep the other leg grounded to maintain balance.
  • Overextending the leg: Keep the circles small and controlled, especially if you're a beginner.
