Leg Lift/h4>

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Lie on your side with your legs stacked and extended, head resting on your lower arm.
  • Engage your core and keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  • Inhale and lift the top leg towards the ceiling, keeping it straight and aligned with your body.
  • Exhale as you lower the leg back down without letting it touch the bottom leg.
  • Repeat for 10-15 repetitions, then switch sides.

What to Avoid When Doing the Bridge Yoga Pose
  • Rolling the hips: Keep your hips stacked and facing forward to maintain proper alignment.
  • Using momentum: Focus on controlled movements rather than swinging your leg.
  • Letting the bottom leg lift: Keep the bottom leg anchored on the mat for stability.
  • Holding your breath: Breathe evenly throughout the exercise for better control.
