The One Leg Stretch

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the mat.
  • Lift your head, neck, and shoulders off the mat, engaging your core.
  • Extend one leg straight out while keeping the other knee pulled toward your chest.
  • Place your hands on the thigh of the bent leg, and gently pull it closer while extending the other leg.
  • Switch legs, maintaining a smooth and controlled motion. Repeat for 5â€“10 repetitions on each side.

What to Avoid When Doing the Bridge Yoga Pose
  • Straining the neck: Keep your neck relaxed and avoid pushing your head forward.
  • Arching the lower back: Engage your core to keep your lower back pressed into the mat.
  • Holding your breath: Breathe steadily throughout the exercise.
  • Rushing the movement: Focus on slow and controlled leg extensions for better engagement.
