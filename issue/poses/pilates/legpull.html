Leg Pulls

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Start in a plank position with your hands under your shoulders and your body in a straight line.
  • Engage your core and lift one leg off the ground, keeping it straight and in line with your body.
  • Hold the position for a moment, then lower the leg back to the starting position.

What to Avoid When Doing the Bridge Yoga Pose
  • Letting the hips sag: Keep your hips level and body straight throughout the exercise.
  • Holding your breath: Maintain steady breathing while lifting and lowering the legs.
  • Shifting weight: Keep your weight evenly distributed between your hands and feet.
  • Rushing the movement: Focus on controlled lifts to maximize effectiveness.
