Uttanasana(Standing Forward Bend)

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Start Position: Stand with your feet hip-width apart and arms at your sides.
  • Hinge Forward: On an exhale, hinge at your hips and fold forward, bringing your hands towards the floor or your shins.
  • Relax Your Neck: Allow your head to hang heavy and relax your neck, keeping your gaze towards your legs or the floor.
  • Hold the Pose: Hold the forward bend for 15 to 30 seconds, breathing deeply and evenly.
  • Release: To come out of the pose, engage your core, bend your knees slightly, and slowly rise up to a standing position.

What to avoid while doing this pose
  • Keep a slight bend in your knees to avoid straining your lower back.
  • Avoid rounding your lower back; hinge from your hips and keep your spine long.
  • If you feel any discomfort or strain, ease out of the pose and use props for support as needed.
