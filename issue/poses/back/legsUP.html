Legs Up the Wall (Viparita Karani)

To practice this pose, follow the instructions-

  • Start Position: Sit next to a wall, then lie down on your back while swinging your legs up against the wall.
  • Adjust Your Position: Ensure your buttocks are close to the wall, and your legs are straight up. Your arms can rest at your sides or on your belly.
  • Relax: Close your eyes and focus on your breath. Stay in this position for 5 to 15 minutes.
  • To Exit: Gently bend your knees and roll to your side. Take a moment before getting up to a seated position.

What to avoid while doing this pose
  • Avoid this pose if you have any neck or back injuries. Use a bolster or blanket under your hips for support if needed.
  • If you feel any discomfort in your legs or back, ease out of the pose.
  • Consult with a healthcare professional if you are pregnant or have specific health concerns.
